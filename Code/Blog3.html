CASE STUDY : SCOOP MY ART'S INSTAGRAM PAGE

By Rasheeque Ibnu Abdul Latheef15 Aug 2020

About the Company

Scoop My Art is a startup that is in the development phase. They plan to develop an online marketplace, which is a platform for people to buy and sell homemade and handpicked products. They started their Instagram page in September 2018 to build a hub for people who love unique stuff. In the Instagram page, they promote such homepreneurs by giving shoutouts and business tips to them.

The problem faced by our client

They were not happy with the rate at which followers were increasing, they were hardly getting 100 likes per post and impressions were not improving. Also, they wanted to create an intro website which must be kind of a promo for the actual online marketplace, and attractive enough that sellers register for the website.

Goals set by us

After a discussion with the clients, we set out our goals first so that we could plan out what strategy to use. Goals were as follows:

  • Increase Organic followers and create more brand awareness
  • Increase Impressions per Post
  • Make the posts more engaging
  • Develop a Website and increase traffic to the website

Market Study

We started interacting with the followers as to what kind of posts they like, what are the pain points they are facing, and how Scoop My Art can help them. We did a detailed study of the audience as to what category of people were following Scoop My Art, starting from their age, interests, hobbies to incomes. We also started asking people as to what were their expectations from a page like Scoop My Art.

Our Strategy

Planned out the theme and type of content for the page

  • Weekly Tips : Our client was already providing advice and tips to the homepreneurs and they loved these tips. So we thought of promoting these tips as an Instagram Post and thus introduced the productive Tips. We saw that they had maximum engagement on Saturdays at 12 pm and thus that was the time chosen to post these weekly tips.
  • Promoted Brand Message : Our clients are super quick at responding to messages. They believe that a seller must be replied within an hour and they follow this. So we decided to start as a challenge as to whoever does not get a reply within an hour will get a gift. And to take part in the challenge messages started pouring in. This way many people started interacting with the page.
  • Shoutout Week : Once in a while we organized shoutout weeks to promote these homepreneurs in our stories. We dedicated one day for each category such as accessories, cakes, handcrafts etc.
  • Yellow Tips : So these tips started as an experiment to give a special theme to the page wherein each day they would have 2 regular promotion posts and 1 Yellow Tip. such that all the yellow tips are aligned in the same line. The yellow color was to represent the brand color of Scoop My Art and the tips revolved around solving the pain points which a homepreneur generally faced.

Hashtag Strategy

We noticed that the reach was stagnant and there was no improvement even though we were using many hashtags. Thatâ€™s when we decided to refine the hashtag strategy.

  • We decided to target a mix of specific hashtags as well as a few generic ones.
  • The number of posts in a hashtag that we choose should not exceed a certain number
  • Hashtags should not be repetitive and must keep changing

Results

And now we present the results that we saw in these 6 months

Huge increase in likes and comments especially for the Weekly Tips and Yellow Tips. The followers loved it and engaged a lot with these posts.

The impression per post shot up by 215%, thanks to our customized hashtag strategy.

Their number of followers increased by 70%.

The visitors to the website increased by 301%.
