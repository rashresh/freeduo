HOW TO BUILD A SOCIAL MEDIA PRESENCE FROM SCRATCH?

By Reshmi Menon15 Aug 2020

Image Source: Freepik

Be it a business or your personal profile, if you are starting your social media account for monetary gains or to offer value, there are few tips and tricks you need to keep in mind to build your social media account.

Set your goals, values and mission statements

Just like any business, your social media should have a clear plan as to what kind of services you will offer and how you are going to treat your partners or customers. What is the mission of the page? What are your goals and how do you plan to achieve it? Someone who visits your Social Media Account should not get the vibe that you are all over the place and are aimless.

State clearly as to what you offer - have a portfolio or brochure

It is great if you have a portfolio or brochure as to the kind of services you offer. You can also mention the price, benefits, How-Tos, everything in detail. Try to make it attractive and mention your value proposition clearly. Instead of focussing on the services you provide let the customers know what are the benefits they get out of it!!

Choose a platform that suits you

You don’t need to be active across all platforms and channels. If you are good at shooting videos or presentations focus on those platforms, if you write great blogs then you can choose that medium to build your brand. You can always experiment, but if that method is not working, then learn to pivot or move on.

Do not hesitate to message all prospective clients

If you find a prospective client, you can always send across a message greeting them and telling them about your service and how it can help them. Keep the message brief and to the point, be polite and do not upsell or market yourself. Just talk about what offer you can give to them. But never spam them.

Reply quickly to all messages and comments

Never miss out on any messages or comments, because this proves how much you value a person who engages with you. And also try to do it quickly so that they know you are quick at your job.

Be human

Never use bots to reply to comments and messages. Do not send across drafted messages. Always be interactive and empathetic to your customers or followers. Let them know that they are talking to a human and not a robot.

Maintain theme and consistency

All the social media platforms use algorithms, and one way to be a part of the algorithm is to maintain consistency. Try to post as frequently as possible. 3 times a week is the minimum which must follow for sure. I suggest daily 2 to 3 posts. The more active you are, the more others will see your content and you will stay in their visibility. Also, it’s great to have a theme, be it the way your feed looks or the way your content is presented. Let it not waver away from your actual goal.

Stay updated about the new trends and showcase that in Social Media

This is the best way to keep your followers engaged. Be it fun memes, news, a new feature, or any other trend in your industry, creating amazing posts, and share it across your platforms.

Use scheduling and Analytical tools

So what’s the use of the content if it is not getting the right traffic? Do not hesitate to use authorized automated tools. You can schedule posts as per the time you get the most engagement and there are tons of tools to analyze as to the type of audience who interact with your content, their age, location, and interests, etc.

So basically you should not be afraid to try our new things and jump out of your comfort zone. Create a plan, set goals, evaluate, and improvise. This should be your mantra
